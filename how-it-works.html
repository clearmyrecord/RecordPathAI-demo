How RecordPathAI Works

RecordPathAI guides you through the record clearing process by helping you check eligibility, organize your case information, and prepare your filing packet.

1

Check Your Eligibility

Start by answering a few questions about your record, charges, case outcome, and timing. This helps determine whether you may qualify for record sealing or expungement.

2

Build Your Record Profile

Enter your case details so your information is structured correctly. RecordPathAI helps organize the charges, dates, and court-related information needed to move forward.

3

Prepare Your Filing Packet

Based on the information you provide, RecordPathAI prepares the packet details and documents you need to continue the record clearing process with the court.

4

Move Forward with the Court

Review your packet carefully and follow the filing steps required by your court. Depending on the court, you may need to print, sign, mail, or file documents in person.
