How RecordPathAI Works

RecordPathAI helps you move through the record clearing process step by step by collecting your information, organizing your case details, reviewing possible eligibility, and preparing a cleaner filing workflow before you go to court.

1

Start With Your Intake Information

The process begins with your personal and case-related intake information. This gives RecordPathAI the foundation it needs to build your filing workflow and prepare information that may later be used in your packet.

Your intake is designed to collect the user information needed throughout the workflow, including the case state that controls which rules and court path apply.
This step is meant to keep the process organized from the beginning so your information carries forward instead of being re-entered over and over.
2

Enter and Organize Your Record Details

Next, you move into your record details. This is where charges, outcomes, dates, counts, and related case information are organized in a more structured way so the platform can review the record more accurately.

RecordPathAI is being built to support smarter data entry with guided fields, organized case details, and charge-focused workflows instead of confusing legal paperwork first.
As the platform expands, this step supports cleaner offense and disposition data that can be used for eligibility review, packet logic, and court-specific preparation.
3

Review Eligibility on the Packet Page

RecordPathAI does not stop at intake. Your eligibility review is determined after your information and record details are brought together on the packet page, where the platform can evaluate the record in a more complete context.

This includes eligibility status messaging, review flags, and timing-based guidance such as estimated eligibility dates when waiting periods may apply.
Because rules can vary by state and case type, RecordPathAI is designed to follow the case state and use that as the basis for packet logic and next-step guidance.
4

Prepare Your Filing Packet

Once your information is organized and reviewed, RecordPathAI helps prepare your packet workflow. That includes bringing together the information needed for court forms, packet summaries, and filing-related preparation.

The platform is being built to support document preparation, official-form workflows, and packet generation that is cleaner, faster, and more consistent than starting from scratch.
Where available, RecordPathAI is also being designed to support signature collection, packet review, and structured outputs that help users understand what comes next before filing.
5

Follow the Right Court Path

After your packet is prepared, RecordPathAI helps you understand the next step in the process. Depending on the court and state, that may include printing, signing, mailing, filing in person, or reviewing additional local requirements before submission.

The platform is being expanded to support court guidance, filing checklists, lookup helpers, and court-specific preparation so users have a clearer path instead of guessing.
Reminder features and checklist tools can help users stay on track when they are not yet eligible today or still need to complete part of the process before filing.

What RecordPathAI Helps With

RecordPathAI is designed to reduce confusion, improve organization, and make record clearing workflows easier to understand before users deal with court paperwork on their own.

Structured Intake

Collect and carry forward the personal and case information needed throughout the workflow.

Eligibility Review

Analyze record details, waiting-period timing, and case-state rules in a more complete packet-based review flow.

Packet Preparation

Prepare document workflows, filing guidance, reminders, and next-step support before going to court.

Important

RecordPathAI helps users prepare and organize their record clearing workflow, but court requirements vary by state, county, and court. Some courts may require additional forms, signatures, fingerprints, filing fees, hearings, or local steps before a case can be filed or completed. RecordPathAI is designed to improve preparation and guidance, but users should review all court requirements carefully before submitting anything.
