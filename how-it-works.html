How It Works:
Automatic Criminal Record Clearing

Clear your record fast in just 3 simple steps.

1

1. Check Your Eligibility

Answer a few simple questions about your record, charges, case outcome, and timing. Our system helps screen whether you may qualify for record sealing or expungement.

2

2. We Generate Your Forms

If you may qualify, ClearMyRecord helps organize and generate the information needed for court forms and packet preparation so everything is easier to review before filing.

3

3. Prepare to File

Review your forms, required details, and court-specific next steps before filing. Some courts may require printing, signatures, attachments, or fingerprints.
