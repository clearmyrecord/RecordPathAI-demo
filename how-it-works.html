How ClearMyRecord Works

ClearMyRecord helps people understand whether they may qualify, what information they need, and how to get organized before filing court paperwork.

Simple steps to get started.

The process is designed to be straightforward. You answer questions, organize case information, and prepare the details needed for forms and packet creation.

🔎

Start with a short screening to review charges, case outcomes, and timing. This helps determine whether you may qualify to move forward.

3. Prepare to File

What the platform helps you do

Organize case information

ClearMyRecord helps structure names, courts, case numbers, charge details, outcomes, and dates so users can gather everything needed before moving deeper into the filing process.

Prepare form-ready details

The system is built to help users collect and format the information commonly needed for court forms and filing packets, even when requirements vary by court.

Understand court requirements

Some courts require extra steps, such as fingerprints, mailing copies, attachments, or local cover sheets. The goal is to help users prepare before they get to the courthouse.

Move faster with more confidence

Instead of starting with confusion, users can begin with a structured process that makes it easier to know what to do next and what information may still be missing.
