What ClearMyRecord Does

  • Guides users through the application process step by step
  • Stores intake data like contact info, mailing address, and charge details
  • Connects user data to court form templates
  • Helps prepare filing packets for sealing and expungement workflows
  • Builds toward reminders, court lookup, and official-form generation

Why this matters

Millions of people may be eligible for record relief and never file. Some do not know they qualify. Some cannot afford an attorney. Others get overwhelmed by paperwork, deadlines, or court procedures.

How ClearMyRecord works

The platform is built to move a user from uncertainty to action.

Step 1

Start the application

The user enters contact information, mailing address, and charge details to begin the process.

Step 2

Review eligibility signals

The application compares charge data, disposition details, and wait times to determine potential eligibility.

Step 3

Build the filing packet

Saved data can be mapped into court templates and assembled into a packet for filing.
